Privacy Policy — Saath

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Saath in one line: Saath is an educational legal companion that helps people in India understand and navigate their legal journey in plain language. Saath provides educational guidance, not legal advice, and is not a substitute for a qualified lawyer.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Saath mobile application ("Saath", "the app", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Saath, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account details — when you sign up, we collect your name, phone number and/or email address so we can create and secure your account.
  • Case information — details you enter about your legal matter (such as case type, stage, notes and reminders) so the app can guide you.
  • Documents you scan or upload — when you use the document decoder, the photo or file you provide, and the text extracted from it, are processed to generate a plain-language explanation.

Information collected automatically

  • Location — with your permission, your approximate or precise location is used to find nearby legal aid offices, One Stop Centres, or courthouses. Location is used only when you ask for it and is not tracked in the background.
  • Device & usage information — basic technical data (device type, app version, and error logs) needed to keep the app working and to fix problems.

Device permissions we request

  • Camera — to scan and decode legal documents.
  • Photos / files — so you can choose an existing document to decode.
  • Location — to find the nearest legal help.
  • Microphone — for voice input, where offered.
  • Notifications — to remind you about hearing dates, deadlines and tasks.

You can grant or deny each permission, and change it later in your phone's settings. Denying a permission only disables the related feature.

2. How we use your information

  • To create your account and keep it secure.
  • To provide the app's features — guidance, document decoding, reminders and finding legal help.
  • To personalise the journey and checklists to your case.
  • To fix bugs, prevent misuse, and improve the app.
  • To comply with legal obligations that apply to us.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Automated / AI processing

To explain documents and generate guidance, the text from documents you submit may be processed by automated systems, including AI language models operated by us or by trusted service providers acting on our behalf. These explanations are for understanding only and are not legal advice. Please avoid submitting information you do not wish to be processed for this purpose.

4. How your information is stored and shared

  • On your device: much of your case and document data is stored locally on your phone.
  • Our backend: account and synced data are stored using our hosting and database provider, Supabase, which processes data on our behalf under its own security and privacy terms.
  • Service providers: we share information only with providers who help us run the app (such as hosting, database and AI processing), and only as needed to deliver the service.
  • Legal reasons: we may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights and safety of users and the public.

5. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures — including encrypted connections and secure storage for sensitive values — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may delete your data or ask us to delete it (see "Your rights"). Some information may be retained where required to meet legal obligations or resolve disputes.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Access the personal information associated with your account.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw permissions at any time through your phone settings.

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's privacy

Saath is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
